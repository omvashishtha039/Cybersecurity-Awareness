Beware of Phishing Attacks

Phishing attacks are fraudulent attempts to steal sensitive information such as passwords, credit card details, or personal data. These attacks often come in the form of emails, texts, or websites that look legitimate but are designed to deceive. Learning how to identify and avoid phishing is critical to keeping your information safe and secure online.

Strong Passwords: Your First Line of Defense

Strong passwords are one of the most important defenses against cyber threats. It’s essential to use complex, unique passwords for every account and enable two-factor authentication wherever possible. This page offers practical tips on creating and managing passwords to keep your online accounts secure.

Secure Your Mobile Devices

Mobile device security is crucial as smartphones and tablets are often targets for cybercriminals. Ensure your mobile security by using strong passwords, downloading apps only from trusted sources, and avoiding public Wi-Fi networks. This page provides tips and best practices to help you secure your mobile devices and protect your data on the go.
